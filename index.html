UNACCOMPANIED

The memories of an undocumented journey

Javier Zamora traveled to the USA as an unaccompanied minor when he was nine years old from his home country in El Salvador. Some twenty years later, he has taken it upon himself to revisit his past and retrace his journey, exploring how it impacted his life and how it has changed for those immigrating today, who form part of the largest diaspora in the modern history of the United States.
